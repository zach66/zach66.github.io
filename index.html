Project Statement

The goal of this project is to analyze the estimations of the magnetosphere in order to explore and characterize typical and atypical behavior in the physical system by applying data mining and visualization tools on the predicted electromagnetic density values. Using exploratory data analysis and unsupervised learning methods, we aim to understand normal behavior in the magnetosphere and perhaps detect interesting anomalies for subsequent analysis.

We also aim to develop an application to visualize the results from our analyses to facilitate understanding and to catalyze further exploration of the data.
